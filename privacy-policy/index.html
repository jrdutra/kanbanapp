Last updated: 2026-02-27

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how KanbanApp andd kanbanapp.io handle information when you visit kanbanapp.io.

Website: KanbanApp
URL: https://kanbanapp.io/

KanbanApp is a static website and an offline-first tool that can run directly in your browser. We do not require an account to read the content on the website.

If you contact us (for example via email or a contact form), we may receive the information you choose to share, such as your name, email address, and the content of your message.

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We and our partners may use cookies for:

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We do not control the content or privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of each website you visit.

We use reasonable measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We recommend keeping your browser updated and using trusted networks.

KanbanApp is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

8) Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights related to personal data, such as requesting access, correction, or deletion. To make a request, please contact us via: Contact page.

9) Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10) Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: https://kanbanapp.io/contact/
